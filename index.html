How it works?

The main idea of this project was to let people know how much money they gain
from their business and how it depends between another companies.

Now we`ll tell you how our project works.

First of all you can try to click on button "Custom Data" this button will show the user how an random data works with our project.

If you did that already you can calculate your own data. Here`s your next steps.

First step:

In this step we`ll calculate your profit and rate of income/expenses by your data.

You need to click on first button "Write Your Own Data" and read the next step.

Second step:

You need to write your total income in your business then total expenses and total sold items.

After all of this information you will see your rate of income/expenses and your profit in the tables.

Now you can calculate the rate of an objects that your company sold.

First step:

You need to click on second button "Write Your Own Data" and read the next step.

Second step:

The site will ask you to enter an name of each your product and how many you sold that.

Then you`ll get an ready layout of percentage of your sold items and you`ll know
what product is the most popular from customers in your business.
